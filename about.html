This website is built for trans people.

FB makes you use your "real" name. Twitter suspends people who speak out against TERFS. What's the alternative? Where can we live freely? Genderev is the answer.

We care about the privacy of trans people's lives.

Genderev is built on top of Blockstack, which allows the sharing of data while maintaining decentralization.

  • The only information we can see is what you share publicly.
  • No tracking, ever.
  • When you use Genderev, you own your data.

It's just beautiful.
